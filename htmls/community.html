The Ajay Experience Community Commitment

The Ajay Experience is committed to fostering a space where authenticity thrives through music, dance, and community. Authenticity flourishes when vulnerability is embraced, and we strive to create an environment where everyone feels secure, valued, and encouraged to express themselves freely.

We kindly ask our patrons and audience members to uphold a space where respect and protection are extended to all members of our diverse community and their unique identities. Our events are designed to foster unity and a strong sense of belonging, celebrating culture and joy through music.

Zero-Tolerance Policy

THE AJAY EXPERIENCE UNEQUIVOCALLY CONDEMNS ALL FORMS OF ASSAULT, LACK OF CONSENT, HATE SPEECH, AND BIGOTRY, AS WELL AS RACISM, CLASSISM, SEXISM, AGEISM, HOMOPHOBIA, TRANSPHOBIA, AND ANY OTHER FORMS OF DISCRIMINATION OR PREJUDICE WITHIN OUR SPACE.

Additionally, we maintain zero tolerance for actions including but not limited to physical, sexual, psychological, verbal, or socio-economic violence.

The Ajay Experience reserves the right to deny entry or remove any person whose actions negatively impact others in our space.

Media Consent Agreement

By purchasing a ticket to The Ajay Experience, you agree to be photographed and filmed at the event. These visuals may be used for promotional purposes, including social media, marketing materials, and future event promotions.
